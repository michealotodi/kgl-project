Company Rules & Regulations

  • Only products available in stock should be sold. Once it is out of stock, the manager should be notified.
  • For any sold produce, its tonnage is reduced automatically.
  • Prices are determined by the manager and are pre-set in the system.
  • No sales agent is allowed to record any produce entry.
  • Only the manager can record procurement and also manage produce types, prices, and dealers.
  • The manager can also record a sale.
  • Only the director, Mr. Micheal, is allowed to view sales made in all branches (summary only).
