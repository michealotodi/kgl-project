Karibu Groceries LTD - Company Rules

Here, you can keep track of procurement, monitor your stock, manage sales, and ensure accurate records for every transaction.

📝 General Rules

  • ✅ All employees must report to work by 8:00 AM.
  • ✅ Professional behavior is expected at all times.
  • ✅ No unauthorized persons are allowed in restricted areas.
  • ✅ Use of company resources for personal gain is prohibited.
  • ✅ All procurement and sales must be logged through the system daily.
  • ✅ Only products available in stock should be sold. Managers must be notified if out of stock.
  • ✅ Sold produce automatically reduces tonnage from stock.
  • ✅ Prices are set by managers and pre-configured in the system.
  • ✅ Sales agents cannot record produce entries.
  • ✅ Only managers can record procurement and manage produce types, prices, and dealers.
  • ✅ Managers can also record sales.
  • ✅ Only the director, Mr. Micheal, can view branch-wide sales summaries.

⏰ Working Hours

  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed
